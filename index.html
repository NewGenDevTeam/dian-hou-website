Born from

Dian Huo was born from a simple belief — that the best meals happen in the open air, under a darkening sky, with the heat of a hotpot between you and the people you love. Perched on Level 3, we built Petaling Jaya's most memorable rooftop table.

Why Dian Huo · 為何選擇我們

The experience
in three acts.

01

Open-Air Rooftop

Dine beneath the Petaling Jaya skyline on our open-air Level 3 terrace. Every table faces the city lights — a view that turns dinner into a moment.

02

Signature Broths

Eight house-crafted broths, from our slow-simmered Coconut Herbal to the signature Spicy Mala. Each pot is a recipe honed over hundreds of sittings.

03

Premium Cuts

Handmade balls, wagyu slices, and fresh seafood — sourced daily. Every ingredient on our table earns its place in the pot.
