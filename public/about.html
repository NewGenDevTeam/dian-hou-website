The Beginning

WHERE FIRE
MEETS SKY

點火心窩 was born from one idea: the ancient communal ritual of hotpot, taken eighteen floors above the city, where the broth bubbles under an open sky and the conversation flows as freely as the steam.

Every broth simmers for twelve hours. Every ingredient is sourced that morning. Every seat looks out at the city you call home — because some meals deserve more than four walls.
